- Frequency entrainment for rapid focus and recall.
- HUMINT-derived observation drills for situational awareness.
- Safety gates and tie-down procedures to maintain cognitive hygiene.

## Universal Protocol

| Step | Duration | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Preparation** | 5 min | Set environment, regulate breathing, choose base frequency. |
| **Induction** | 10 min | Anchor on sacred geometry visual, stabilize at 10 Hz. |
| **Training** | 20-30 min | Alternate task-specific beats, capture notes in logbook. |
| **Integration** | 10 min | Shift toward 6 Hz theta, summarise insights aloud. |
| **Exit** | 5 min | Return to beta range, perform grounding exercise. |

## Safety Reminders

- Hydrate before and after sessions.
- Maintain a physical grounding object within reach.
- Pause immediately if disorientation or discomfort emerges.
